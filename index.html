About Xipton Group

Who We Are

Xipton Group (U) Ltd is a trusted agricultural company in Uganda, dedicated to coffee parchment processing and sustainable dairy farming. We combine traditional agricultural wisdom with modern processing technologies to deliver high-quality products and sustain rural livelihoods.

We work closely with local farmers to ensure traceability, quality, and fair compensation — building resilient supply chains and stronger communities.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To add value to Uganda’s agricultural products while promoting sustainable farming and improving rural livelihoods.

Vision: To be a leading force in sustainable coffee processing and dairy farming in East Africa.

Our Divisions

Coffee Parchment Processing

We process coffee parchment to strict quality standards — from sorting and drying to grading. Our processing plant employs hygienic, efficient techniques to preserve the unique aroma and flavor profiles of Ugandan coffee.

We partner with smallholder farmers, provide fair procurement terms, and maintain traceability so buyers can trust the origin of every bag.

Dairy Farming

Our dairy unit focuses on animal welfare, modern feeding, and milk quality. We implement best practices in herd management to produce nutritious milk for local markets and processors.

We also run training programs that enable youth and women to participate in livestock farming as sustainable livelihoods.

Sustainability & Impact
